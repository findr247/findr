Find quick answers to the most common questions about reporting, matching, and reclaiming lost items on our platform. We're here to make the process simple and efficient for you.

How do I report a lost or found item?

Simply click on the "Report Item" button, fill out the form with all relevant details (description, location, photos), and submit it. Your report will be added to our database for matching.

What happens after I submit a report?

Our system immediately analyzes your report and cross-references it with other entries in the database. You’ll receive notifications if a potential match is found.

How does the matching process work?

Our smart algorithm scans descriptions, locations, and photos to identify possible matches. Once a match is detected, you’ll be notified to verify the item.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security. Your information is only used for matching purposes and is never shared without your consent.

What if I find an item but don’t know the owner?

You can still submit a "found" report. Provide as many details as possible, and our platform will help connect the item with its rightful owner.

Would you like to recover your lost items or help someone find theirs?
